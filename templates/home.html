About

Welcome to Talk2Text, your intelligent companion for academic success. We are a passionate team of developers and learners committed to simplifying the way students interact with lecture content. Our platform automatically transcribes lectures—whether uploaded or sourced from YouTube—and transforms them into clean, summarized notes with important keywords highlighted. Beyond just transcription, our tool leverages the power of AI to extract key points, generate intelligent flashcards, and present content in an organized, easy-to-review format. Whether you're revising for exams or trying to catch up on missed lectures, our goal is to help you learn smarter, not harder. We believe in the power of technology to make education more accessible, efficient, and engaging for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the transcription?
We use the Whisper model, which provides highly accurate transcriptions even with moderate background noise.
Can I upload any audio or video format?
Currently, we support MP3, WAV for audio and MP4 for videos. YouTube URLs are also supported for transcription.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and do not share or sell any user data.
